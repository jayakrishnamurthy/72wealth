About 72Wealth

72Wealth, a brand of Pro72 Services Private Limited, is a SEBI-registered Mutual Fund Distributor established in 2018. We are an independent financial advisory firm dedicated to providing evidence-based wealth management solutions that align with the financial objectives and life circumstances of our clients.

Our firm was founded on a fundamental belief: investors deserve unbiased, research-driven advice grounded in institutional best practices. This principle emerged from our founders' extensive experience managing investment portfolios and navigating multiple market cycles across the financial services industry.

Mission

To enable wealth creation and financial security for Indian investors through disciplined, research-driven investment guidance aligned with individual goals and delivered with integrity.

Vision

Building sustainable financial security for all Indians through evidence-based investment advice, institutional-grade analysis, and a commitment to advancing investor confidence in the mutual fund ecosystem.

Our Founding Philosophy

The 72Wealth team comprises investment industry professionals with deep expertise in fund management, equity analysis, and investment strategy. Our founders have held leadership positions at recognized asset management companies, where they managed institutional portfolios and developed sophisticated investment evaluation frameworks.

This institutional background provides us with a distinctive advantage: we evaluate mutual fund schemes through the rigorous analytical lens of professional fund managers, rather than relying on marketing narratives or consensus rankings. We apply the same diligence standards we would use for institutional capital to individual investor portfolios.

Our approach recognizes that successful wealth creation requires:

  • Evidence-based selection grounded in quantitative and qualitative analysis
  • Alignment with individual goals rather than performance chasing
  • Downside protection focus across market cycles
  • Consistent methodology applied with intellectual honesty
  • Transparent communication about investment rationale and risks

Our Investment Philosophy

At 72Wealth, our investment philosophy rests on several foundational principles:


  • Client-Centric Approach: Every recommendation is structured around your specific financial situation, objectives, and constraints—not around market trends or available products.

  • Research-Driven Analysis: We employ systematic evaluation frameworks that combine quantitative metrics (risk-adjusted returns, downside volatility, benchmark consistency) with qualitative assessment (fund management philosophy, investment process consistency, organizational stability).

  • Downside-Focused Evaluation: We prioritize capital preservation during market corrections. We recognize that protecting wealth during adverse market conditions is as important as generating returns during favorable periods.

  • Institutional Discipline: Our methodology reflects the rigorous analytical standards applied by professional asset managers. We maintain consistency in evaluation criteria and decision-making processes across all recommendations.

  • Ongoing Monitoring: Our advisory relationship continues after implementation. We monitor portfolio performance, rebalance in accordance with predetermined criteria, and adjust positioning when circumstances change or investment theses are invalidated.
