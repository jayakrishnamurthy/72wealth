What is a Mutual Fund?

A mutual fund is a professionally managed investment vehicle that pools money from many investors to purchase a diversified basket of securities like stocks, bonds, and gold.

Key Benefit: It allows individual investors access to professionally managed portfolios that would otherwise be difficult to build on an individual basis.

Why Choose Mutual Funds?

  • Professional Management: Expert fund managers research and manage your investments.
  • Diversification: Spreading risk across multiple sectors and companies.
  • Affordability: Start with as little as ₹500 via SIP.
  • Liquidity: Most funds allow you to withdraw money on any business day.
