How to Start Your Journey

Starting your investment journey is a streamlined, digital-first process. Follow these five essential steps to begin.

1

Complete Your KYC

Submit your PAN, Aadhaar, and bank details. Digital KYC can be completed instantly via OTP verification.

2

Define Your Goals

Decide if you are saving for a short-term vacation (1-2 years) or a long-term goal like retirement (10+ years).

3

Choose Investment Mode

Pick SIP for disciplined monthly investing or Lumpsum for one-time surplus deployment.

4

Select a Scheme

Evaluate funds based on their expense ratio and long-term consistency rather than just recent returns.

5

Monitor Regularly

Review your portfolio every 6-12 months with your advisor to ensure you stay on track.
